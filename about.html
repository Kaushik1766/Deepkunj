About Us

DeepKunj Sarvojanin Puja Samiti, established in the auspicious year of 1988, stands as a beacon of devotion and community spirit. With a commitment to preserving and perpetuating our rich cultural heritage, we proudly organize four major pujas - Durga Puja, Kali Puja, Lakshmi Puja, and Saraswati Puja, each representing a unique facet of our faith and tradition.

Our journey of faith and togetherness is etched in the very essence of our community. Our registration number, 3558, symbolizes our commitment to transparency and adherence to legal standards.

DeepKunj is more than just a place of worship; it's a space where hearts unite, traditions thrive, and bonds are forged. We invite you to explore our website to discover the significance of each puja, stay informed about our upcoming events, and become a part of our growing family. Join us in celebrating the divine and nurturing the cultural tapestry that binds us together.

Our Mission

At DeepKunj Sarvojanin Puja Samiti, our mission transcends the mere celebration of pujas. We are dedicated to fostering a sense of unity, inclusivity, and spiritual growth within our community. Our pujas serve as a bridge between tradition and modernity, bringing together people from diverse backgrounds to embrace our shared cultural heritage.

Community Engagement

We believe that true spirituality is not confined to the temple walls alone. Our Samiti actively engages in various social and cultural activities, contributing to the betterment of society. Through initiatives such as educational programs, health camps, and charity drives, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of those around us.

Preserving Tradition

DeepKunj Samiti is committed to preserving the traditions and rituals that have been passed down through generations. With each puja, we offer a platform for learning and experiencing the essence of our customs, ensuring that our cultural heritage remains vibrant and relevant.

Get Involved

We welcome individuals of all ages and backgrounds to join us in our journey. Whether you're a long-standing member or a newcomer, your presence and participation enrich our celebrations. Together, we can continue to strengthen our bonds, celebrate our faith, and serve our community.
